Projects

Kalavoor Residence

Owners: Mr Mukesh Shanker and Mrs Manju Sathyan
Location: Kalavoor, Alleppey Built-up area: 2300 sqft

The earthy palette rendered by the tiled roof, exposed brick walls and wood is framed by the black steel structure. Light strongly motivated the design language. The louvred doors on the southern walls and jalli patterns with hollow bricks and glass tiles create a dramatic play of light throughout the day. At night, the lights and colours from the interiors under the starry sky and cocooned by the sounds of nature, create a dramatic stage for life to play out.
